FORMATIONS TASK FORCES DATA FILE - Captain Kwok's Balance Mod v125+
=======================================================================================

Controls the position of ships within individual task forces. Formations_Fleet.txt
controls the layout of a fleet's taskforces relative to each other.

(L) = Large formations are designed for TF sizes up to 25-50 ships with a grid size of 25 units (km).
(M) = Medium formations are designed for TF sizes up to 15-25 ships with a grid size of 15 units (km).
(S) = Extra small formations are designed for TF sizes up to 15 ships with a grid size of 15 units (km).
